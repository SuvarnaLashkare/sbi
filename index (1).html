Banking Security Tips

Never Share OTP

SBI or its representatives will never ask for your OTP, PIN, or password. Never share these details with anyone.

Verify Website URL

Always check that you're on the official SBI website before entering your credentials. Look for 'https' and the lock icon.

Use Strong Passwords

Create strong, unique passwords for your banking accounts and change them regularly.
